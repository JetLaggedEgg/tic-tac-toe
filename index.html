How to play:
This is a standard tic-tac-toe game, just online. Players take it in turn to place their tiles on the board. Your goal is to get three in a row, if you do, you will then get a point. You must also block your opponent from doing so themselves. The winner goes first on the next round.


About this game:
This Tic-Tac-Toe game was created using HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. It is currently at version 0.0.8 so may have a few bugs (I got rid of most of them though!).

If you do any find bugs, please email me on daniel.sarracayo@gmail.com with the subject of "Bug in Tic-Tac-Toe!", also including a small description about the bug and any other suggestion in the body of the email. Thanks -Dan.
