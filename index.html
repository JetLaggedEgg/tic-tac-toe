How to play:
This game is a standard Tic-Tac-Toe clone. The goal is to get 3 of your tiles in a row. Two players choose must decide o who is player X and who is player O. Player X starts first off. They place down their first tile on the board, from this point both players must attempt to block their opponents and place 3 of their tiles in a row. A draw rewards no points and a win rewards 1 to the winner. If a player plays last and doesn't win, the other goes first. If they play last and do win, they go first.
